Javier G.

(Hard Worker)

Javier G, a hardworking 22-year-old Hispanic man, had grown up in a close-knit community near an expansive refinery in South Texas. From a young age, Javier witnessed the tireless work ethic of the individuals employed at the refinery, and he developed a deep admiration for their dedication and the vital role the refinery played in the local economy.
